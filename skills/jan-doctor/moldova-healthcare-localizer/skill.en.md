# Moldova Healthcare Localizer

Use this skill when the user needs practical healthcare information specific to Moldova: hospitals, pharmacies, doctors, clinics, prices, contact details, or local processes.

## Goal

Provide accurate, actionable, Moldova-specific healthcare logistics information by scanning and analyzing local sources.

## Local sources to use

- **Hospitals and clinics** — public and private institutions in Chișinău and across Moldova.
- **Pharmacies** — pharmacy chains and independent drug stores, including 24-hour locations.
- **Official portals** — Ministry of Health, National Health Insurance Company (CNAM), hospital websites.
- **Forums and communities** — local patient forums, social groups, review platforms.
- **News** — recent local health news, medicine shortages, price changes, or policy updates.

## Steps

1. **Clarify the request** — location (city/region), service needed (doctor specialty, pharmacy, procedure), urgency, and budget constraints.
2. **Search local sources** — use web search to find current information from Moldovan websites, maps, directories, and official portals.
3. **Verify details** — cross-check addresses, phone numbers, working hours, and prices across at least two sources when possible.
4. **Summarize options** — list relevant hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, or doctors with:
   - Name
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Working hours
   - Services or specialties
   - Price range or consultation fees, if available
5. **Explain the process** — how to make an appointment, required documents, insurance coverage, estimated wait times.
6. **Highlight recent changes** — note any known shortages, schedule changes, or price updates from local news.
7. **Cite sources** — link to the portal, directory, or news article used.

## Output format

- **Request summary**: what the user is looking for and where.
- **Recommended options**:
  | Name | Type | Address | Phone | Hours | Notes |
  |---|---|---|---|---|---|
- **Process guidance**: how to access the service.
- **Price information**: known prices or estimates.
- **Source links**: Moldovan websites, portals, or news used.

## Boundaries

- Prefer current, verifiable local sources; avoid outdated information.
- Do not guarantee availability, prices, or appointment slots.
- Always recommend confirming critical details by phone before visiting.
- Remind the user that this is logistical support, not medical advice.
