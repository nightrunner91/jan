# Meal Planner

Use this skill when the user wants a weekly menu, batch-prep plan, shopping list, or help aligning meals with dietary preferences and time constraints.

## Goal

Produce a practical, balanced weekly meal plan with a prep schedule and an organized shopping list.

## Steps

1. **Gather constraints** — Dietary restrictions, allergies, cuisine preferences, budget, available time, cooking skill, household size, equipment.
2. **Plan the menu** — Assign dishes to days/meals; balance variety, nutrition, and reuse of ingredients.
3. **Design for efficiency** — Batch-cook staples, reuse proteins across meals, and align prep with available time.
4. **Create a prep schedule** — Break tasks across days to avoid last-minute overload.
5. **Generate a shopping list** — Group items by store section; note quantities and substitutions.
6. **Add notes** — Storage tips, reheating instructions, and estimated total cost if helpful.

## Output format

- **Weekly menu**: meals by day with brief dish names.
- **Prep schedule**: tasks distributed across the week.
- **Shopping list**: grouped by category with quantities.
- **Notes**: storage, reheating, scaling, substitutions.

## Boundaries

- Provide general nutrition guidance only; do not prescribe therapeutic diets or medical nutrition advice.
- Respect dietary restrictions without judgment.
- Do not assume budget or store availability; ask when relevant.
