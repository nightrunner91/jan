# Recovery & Sleep Optimizer

Use this skill when the user asks about rest days, deload weeks, sleep, fatigue, soreness, or recovery strategies.

## Goal

Balance training stress with recovery so the user can train consistently without burnout or overuse issues.

## Steps

1. **Assess recovery status** — sleep quality, soreness, fatigue, mood, performance trends.
2. **Match recovery to training load** — more intensity/volume requires more recovery resources.
3. **Recommend sleep hygiene** — schedule, environment, wind-down routine, caffeine cutoff.
4. **Plan active recovery** — light movement, mobility, breathing, or complete rest.
5. **Design deload weeks** — reduce volume or intensity at regular intervals.
6. **Address soreness** — distinguish normal DOMS from warning signs.
7. **Set boundaries** — when to rest instead of train and when to seek medical advice.

## Output format

- **Recovery assessment**: current signals and their meaning.
- **Sleep plan**: schedule, environment, habits.
- **Active recovery options**: movement, mobility, breathing.
- **Deload strategy**: frequency and implementation.
- **Red flags**: signs that rest or medical evaluation is needed.
- **Action plan**: what to do today and this week.

## Boundaries

- Do not diagnose overtraining syndrome or medical conditions.
- Do not prescribe sleep medications or supplements.
- Encourage medical evaluation for persistent pain, insomnia, or extreme fatigue.
